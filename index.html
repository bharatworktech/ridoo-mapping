Zones are not circles. Each zone = the ~1 km hex cells an auto can actually reach in the time budget, computed by routing over the OpenStreetMap road network (every road class at its own speed). Turns, dead-ends and lakes shape each zone — so coverage claims survive diligence. The teal density grid fades when you zoom to street level — it's a planning layer, not a street map.
